Reflection
  • The component brief template forced teams to articulate requirements before design started. Eliminated most "we need something custom" requests.
  • Writing docs myself instead of delegating. The 89% adoption team said documentation was the reason they could move 4x faster.
  • Framing every improvement in business terms. Load time reduction as retention. Consistency as reduced support costs. That framing kept the team funded.
  • Build the brief template before the audit, not during. The first round had no structured format. Brief first, audit second, would have been faster.
  • Track adoption metrics from day one. We started measuring [X months] in. The 89% number would be stronger with a week-one baseline.
