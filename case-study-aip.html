Reflection
What worked
  • Killing darlings early — eliminating the full-page search bar before any engineering spend validated the rapid prototyping approach.
  • Framing design decisions in business language: load time became "user retention impact," personalization became "advisor efficiency metrics."
  • 90%+ DS adoption baked into architecture from day one — previous estimate without DS reuse was 18+ months. We shipped in 12.
What I'd do differently
  • Involve content managers in co-design from Phase 1 — content migration became a mid-project bottleneck that an earlier audit would have prevented.
  • Establish shared terminology with engineers upfront — documentation investment would have compressed the development cycle further.
  • Structured 30/60/90 day post-launch check-ins to catch advisor workflow drift before it compounds into backlog debt.
